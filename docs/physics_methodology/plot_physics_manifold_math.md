# Phase Manifold Visualizations

Before any machine learning models are trained, the visualization suite located in `src/visualize/` physically plots the theoretical manifolds generated by the TOV solver.

The framework produces several key projections:
- **The $M-R$ Plane:** This illustrates the classic mass-radius relationship. By plotting thousands of curves simultaneously, we can visually identify the "kink" characteristics and softening effects that differentiate the hadronic models from the self-bound quark models.
- **The $M-\Lambda$ Plane:** This charts the steep inverse scaling of the tidal deformability $\Lambda$ against the stellar mass $M$, highlighting how rapidly finite-size effects vanish as the star approaches the Buchdahl limit.

These plots act as a critical sanity check, allowing us to empirically verify that the anchored $c_s^2$ and Generalized CFL physics engines are generating mathematically viable populations prior to ingestion.
