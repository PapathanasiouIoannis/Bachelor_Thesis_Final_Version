# Topological Phase Manifolds (`src/visualize/`)

The visualization suite generates publication-ready phase manifolds by mapping the integrated solutions from the sequence solver. 

The standard projections include:
- The $M-R$ Plane: Illustrating the mass-radius relationship and the characteristic phase "kinks".
- The $M-\Lambda$ Plane: Detailing the inverse scaling of tidal deformability with increasing mass.

By evaluating these topologies, we visually confirm the rigorous mathematical separation between the generated Hadronic and Quark populations prior to machine learning ingestion.
